Import, export & backup

Everything runs on this device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Export your item list as a CSV or Excel file. In the Excel template, type a Supplier Name that matches one on the "Suppliers" sheet (every supplier already in your account) and the phone number fills in automatically. A brand-new supplier's name just leaves the phone blank for you to fill in — it never overwrites a working lookup. Importing never deletes anything: rows already in your list (same name, unit & supplier) are skipped, everything else is appended.

Export everything — items, suppliers, active and completed orders. Restoring a backup replaces all current data on this device.
