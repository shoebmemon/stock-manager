Import, export & backup

Everything runs on this device — nothing is uploaded anywhere.

Export your item list as a CSV or Excel file — each row carries its supplier's name and phone. In the Excel template, once you've typed a supplier's name once, Excel will suggest it again on later rows and auto-fill the phone number when you pick it. Importing never deletes anything: rows already in your list (same name, unit & supplier) are skipped, everything else is appended.

Export everything — items, suppliers, active and completed orders. Restoring a backup replaces all current data on this device.
